Get to Know Me

I'm a cat mom, and a theatre & concert-lover who enjoys combining creativity with a bit of analytical thinking.

As a UX/UI designer with 3+ years of experience, I specialise in crafting design systems and intuitive interfaces for both web and mobile platforms. My design approach is informed by user research, utilising interviews, usability testing, and other UX research methods.

I create visually engaging graphic designs, including banner sliders and posters, tailored for both digital platforms and print.

With a strong academic foundation in Media and Visual Arts, and my double-major in Sociology, I bring a unique, interdisciplinary approach to design.

Currently, I contribute to the UX/UI design efforts at Koç University's Information Technologies department, as a part of a small UX/UI team. We are a unique team trying to enhance user experience in the higher-education sector!
